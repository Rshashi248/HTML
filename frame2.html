• India Gate is a historical monument situated in the capital of India, New Delhi.

  • It was built to pay homage to the soldiers who fought bravely during World War I.

  • Names of soldiers who were sacrificed during the war are engraved on the monument of India Gate.

  • It took 10 years to build this big monument which was a tribute to the Indian soldiers.

  • Construction of India Gate started on February 1921 and was inaugurated on February 1931.

  • India Gate was designed by Sir Edwin Lutyens which is considered as a masterpiece.

  • After construction of India Gate, a memorial ‘Amar Jawan Jyoti’ was also built afterwards.
